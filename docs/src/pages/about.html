Project Overview

The Digital Clinic Book is a secure, web-based platform designed to replace traditional paper-based clinic books. It enables medical staff and patients to access medical records quickly and safely, with specific access controls based on user roles.

Emergency Access

Provides immediate access to essential medical information during emergencies, including blood type, allergies, chronic conditions, and emergency notes. Emergency access does not require a login.

Patient Accounts

Patients can create an account to securely view their personal medical history, including previous visits, diagnoses, treatments, and prescriptions. Access is strictly limited to the patient’s own data.

Practitioner Accounts

Medical practitioners can log in to access full patient medical records, manage visits, diagnoses, treatments, and prescriptions. All access is authenticated and auditable.

QR Code Integration

Each patient is assigned a unique QR code that can be scanned to access emergency information quickly. Practitioners can also use QR codes to pull full patient records after authentication.

Objectives

  • Digitize clinic records for faster and safer access.
  • Maintain patient privacy and enforce role-based access.
  • Provide emergency medical data without compromising security.
  • Enable audit logging and accountability for all data access.
  • Integrate modern QR code scanning for quick identification.

Technology Stack

  • Frontend: HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Backend & Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL)
  • Authentication: Supabase Auth (email & password)
  • Deployment: Web server (single-machine installation supported)
